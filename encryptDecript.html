Encrypt and Decrypt

This is a simple project that validates email and password as well as encrypt and decrypt password.

Encrypt/Decrypt

This is a simple program where you enter a email and a password. They will both be validated as they are entered. The email will be validated making sure it has a certain amount of charecters before the @ symbol. Also makes sure it has just one @ symbol and one period after @. The password checks if it has a upper case, lower case, number, and a special symbol. Once the email and password are entered the password will be enrypted and then decrypted. The encrypted password will be displayed as well as the decrypted password. If you want to see or download the project you can go to the Repository
